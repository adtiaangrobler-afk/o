About

Welcome to Dead Again! We’re thrilled to have you join us on this spine-tingling adventure.

To get started, here’s a quick rundown of how to play:

  • Move your character using WASD or the arrow keys on your keyboard.
  • Take aim and shoot at those pesky undead creatures using the left mouse button.
  • Use your mouse to adjust your aim and target those ghosts, vampires, and zombies.

Now, let’s talk about the game itself. In Dead Again, you’ll find yourself in a world where the afterlife is far from peaceful. You’ll face a constant onslaught of supernatural foes, from zombies and vampires to malevolent ghosts. The longer you survive, the stronger and more cunning your enemies will become. It’s a real challenge to your survival skills!

But don’t worry, it’s not all doom and gloom. You’ll have a blast navigating through the eerie realms, making split-second decisions, and outsmarting your undead adversaries. And don’t worry if you die – it’s just a temporary setback! Your goal is to see how long you can keep going before succumbing to the inevitable fate of being… well, Dead Again!
